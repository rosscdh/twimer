How do I record my time

Simply send a tweet like:
@minutes_wasted 0:15 #traffic_jam On my way to work! Need more coffee

We record this tweet, and create the #traffic_jam category for future use, as well as store the message for your records.

You can then keep a record of all the time that you "waste"!

 

But I dont want the world to know what I'm spending my time on?

No problem, simply send a tweet like the example above introducing yourself to @minutes_wasted (who will then follow you), and then you can follow @minutes_wasted which lets you direct message your private records.
